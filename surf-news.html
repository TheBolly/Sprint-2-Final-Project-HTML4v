2 Best British Beaches For Waves [RANKED]

Rank 1

One of our favourite surf spots throughout the year would be Bournemouth. Feel free to come down and check it out! If you are new to surfing, that's not a problem. Come down and rent a board, plus sign up for lessons if you wish.

Rank 2

If you are looking for a place to surf during the summer. We suggest the wonderful Fistral Beach! Welcoming all abilities join the many surfers down in Cornwalls Fistral Beach. Enjoy festivals? Come down in August for Boardmasters. You can surf in the day or watch the many surf competitions down at the beach over this week period of surf, sun and music.
